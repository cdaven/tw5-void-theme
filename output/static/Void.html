Void is not a theme, but a starting point for writing a new theme.

The code is based on Zacht, and extends/depends on Vanilla.

How to install:

Drag and drop this link into your TiddlyWiki:

Void: Not a theme 

$:/themes/void

0.1

After installing the theme, change your theme in the control panel to "Void" and your color palette to "Void".
